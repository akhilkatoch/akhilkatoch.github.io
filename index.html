Illustrator / Photoshop/ Titanium

Assist Me App

A Survival Guide for Students. Heading to a higher education can be nerve wrecking. But heading to a newer country for your education can be difficult if you are on the other side of the world. There is a long list of new things to do such as, learning a new language, locating a new place to settle in, figuring out the streets and transit schedules and lastly finding a job to help you pay those bills. The “assist ME” app helps you settle you in your new country/city and school by providing online assistance in regards to everything you need to know about your place, so you can wipe your tears and get to class on time!
